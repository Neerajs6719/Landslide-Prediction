Landslide Causes & Prevention

Causes: Heavy rainfall, earthquakes, deforestation, and steep slopes weaken soil stability, triggering landslides. Human activities like mining or construction can increase risks.

Avoidance: Monitor weather alerts, avoid construction on unstable hillsides, plant trees to reinforce soil, and build proper drainage systems. Stay away from steep areas during storms..
